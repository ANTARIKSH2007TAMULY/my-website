PANCAKES

INGRIDIENTS

  • EGG
  • MILK
  • SUGAR
  • VANILLA ESSENCE
  • CHOCOLATE
  • SPRINKLES

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (or vinegar / curd – acts as buttermilk)
  • 2 tbsp melted butter (or refined oil)
  • 1 egg (or 2 tbsp curd as egg substitute)
HAVE FUN!
